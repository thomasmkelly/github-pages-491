Terms

  • Digital Asset Management Systems
    • A type of CMS. They manage content with clear ownership, such as documents, movies, music, media.
  • Component Content Management Systems
    • Another type of CMS. Used to manage content in a modular fashion. Rather than managing entire articles or pages, a CCMS can be used to manage publications that share content between pages or entries.

The Full Stack Development Process

Full stack development starts by defining the requirements for the project. The site's features, tech stack, database and software plans and schemas, etc. should be identified. Then the site can start to be prototyped. Whether frontend, backend, or both simultaneously. As the site is built out, it can be tested and refined until it reaches an initial deployment-ready state. From here the site can be deployed and made available to its intended users. Improvements, updates, maintenance all continue as the site goes forward.
